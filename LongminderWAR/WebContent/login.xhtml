When was the time for the next dentist checkup? What calendar did I write it again?

Longminder is a tool for long term reminders. Set an alert for some faraway point in the future and when the time comes, you will get a friendly email reminder. Based on the settings you decide, you may have to log on to the service and dismiss the alert, or it will be sent again a few days later.


Some ideas:

  • Biweekly reminder to start reading the book you have sitting on your shelf.
  • Reminder to rotate your bed mattress every 3 months.
  • Reminder every month to pay your recurring bills.
  • Yearly reminder to revise your long term investment strategy.
  • Yearly reminder for medical checkups.
